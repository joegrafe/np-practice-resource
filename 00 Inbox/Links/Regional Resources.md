## Usage Tips
- **RACE Line App:** Download the **RACE App** on your phone. It is faster than calling. Add a "Deep Link" in your Obsidian note (\`raceapp://\`) if possible, or just the phone number: \`1-877-696-2131\`.
- **Pathways:** If you have a login, bookmark the "Specialist Referral" page for your specific region (e.g., "Kelowna - Cardiology").
- **Forms:** Download the **"Section 2 - PWD Designation"** PDF and the **"Special Authority"** PDF from Pharmacare. Keep these in \`docs/assets/forms/\` because you will fill them out weekly.
