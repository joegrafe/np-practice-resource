## Diabetes Canada Clinical Practice Guidelines

**Resource:** [https://guidelines.diabetes.ca/health-care-provider-tools](https://guidelines.diabetes.ca/health-care-provider-tools)

[Diabetes Canada](https://www.diabetes.ca/) provides comprehensive, evidence-based clinical practice guidelines for diabetes prevention, diagnosis, and management. The [guidelines](https://guidelines.diabetes.ca/cpg) consist of 38+ chapters covering all aspects of diabetes care, regularly updated (2024-2025).

### Health Care Provider Tools
- **[Algorithm & Decision Tools](https://guidelines.diabetes.ca/health-care-provider-tools)**: Screening/diagnosis algorithms, management pathways, insulin initiation guides, A1C and glycemic targets
- **Prescribing Guides**: Pharmacologic glycemic management including cardiovascular and renal protection recommendations
- **[Quick Reference Guide](https://guidelines.diabetes.ca/cpgrefguide)**: Condensed PDF with charts for rapid clinical use
- **Case Studies**: Real-world clinical scenarios for complex decision-making
- **[CANRISK Questionnaire](https://www.diabetes.ca/health-care-providers/tools-resources/canrisk)**: Validated diabetes risk assessment tool

### Key Content Areas
- **Type 1 & Type 2 Diabetes Management**: Comprehensive treatment algorithms
- **Complications Management**: Tools for neuropathy, retinopathy, foot care, chronic kidney disease screening
- **Patient Safety**: Hypoglycemia management, sick-day protocols, hospital diabetes care
- **Special Populations**: Children/adolescents, pregnancy and gestational diabetes, elderly, Indigenous peoples
- **Self-Management Support**: Patient education handouts and self-monitoring recommendations
