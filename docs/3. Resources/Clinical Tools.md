## CMA Alternate Providers for Clinical Tools

**Resource:** [https://www.cma.ca/resources/alternate-providers-clinical-tools](https://www.cma.ca/resources/alternate-providers-clinical-tools)

As of December 2023, the CMA no longer provides direct access to clinical tools through membership. This page lists alternate providers and resources for accessing essential clinical decision-support tools.

### Available Alternative Resources
- **[ClinicalKey](https://www.clinicalkey.com/) (Elsevier)**: Comprehensive drug information, clinical guidelines, and multimedia resources (institutional subscription)
- **[CPS](https://www.myrxtx.ca/) (Compendium of Pharmaceuticals and Specialties)**: Authoritative Canadian drug database (group discounts available)
- **[DynaMed](https://www.dynamed.com/)**: Evidence-based point-of-care summaries (institutional/group pricing)
- **[UpToDate](https://www.uptodate.com/)**: Point-of-care evidence summary service (group/institutional discounts via OMA, CFPC)
- **[Essential Evidence Plus](https://www.essentialevidenceplus.com/) & POEMs**: Daily evidence updates and searchable content
- **[CPG Infobase](https://joulecma.ca/cpg/)**: Database of 1,700+ Canadian clinical practice guidelines (no longer updated as of Dec 2023)

### Access Options
- Through hospital/university health authority libraries
- Provincial health system access (many provide ClinicalKey, CPS, Essential Evidence Plus)
- Direct group/institutional subscriptions
- Canadian specialty societies and provincial guideline portals
