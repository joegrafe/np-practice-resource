## CMA Alternate Providers for Clinical Tools

As of December 2023, the CMA no longer provides direct access to clinical tools through membership. This page lists alternate providers and resources for accessing essential clinical decision-support tools.

### Available Alternative Resources
- **ClinicalKey (Elsevier)**: Comprehensive drug information, clinical guidelines, and multimedia resources (institutional subscription)
- **CPS (Compendium of Pharmaceuticals and Specialties)**: Authoritative Canadian drug database (group discounts available)
- **DynaMed**: Evidence-based point-of-care summaries (institutional/group pricing)
- **UpToDate**: Point-of-care evidence summary service (group/institutional discounts via OMA, CFPC)
- **Essential Evidence Plus & POEMs**: Daily evidence updates and searchable content
- **CPG Infobase**: Database of 1,700+ Canadian clinical practice guidelines (no longer updated as of Dec 2023)

### Access Options
- Through hospital/university health authority libraries
- Provincial health system access (many provide ClinicalKey, CPS, Essential Evidence Plus)
- Direct group/institutional subscriptions
- Canadian specialty societies and provincial guideline portals
