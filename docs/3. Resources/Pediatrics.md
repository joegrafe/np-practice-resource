## Canadian Paediatric Society Clinical Tools & Resources

**Resource:** [https://cps.ca/en/tools-outils](https://cps.ca/en/tools-outils)

The [CPS](https://cps.ca/) provides comprehensive, evidence-based clinical practice tools and guidelines for pediatric care professionals in Canada.

### Clinical Tools & Resources
- **Screening & Surveillance Tools**: ADHD, autism spectrum disorder, mental health, growth monitoring, immunization
- **Counselling Guides**: Cannabis use, physical activity, trauma-informed care
- **Printable Resources**: Parent handouts, posters, clinical conversation prompts

### Clinical Practice Guidelines
Key guidelines covering:
- Acute migraine management in children/adolescents
- Pediatric head trauma and well-appearing febrile infants
- Neonatal abstinence syndrome and hyperbilirubinemia
- Cerebral palsy care and asthma management
- Somatic symptom disorders

### Condition-Specific Toolkits
- **[Autism Spectrum Disorder](https://cps.ca/en/tools-outils?filter_category=All&filter_tag=37)**: Surveillance, diagnostic pathways, post-diagnostic support
- **[Mental Health Screening Tools](https://cps.ca/en/tools-outils?filter_category=All&filter_tag=47)**: Validated screening tools for depression, anxiety, behavioral disorders
- **Malnutrition**: E-learning module for assessment/management in hospitalized children
- **Sepsis**: Recognition tools, screening algorithms, care pathways
- **[Pediatric Obesity](https://obesitycanada.ca/guidelines/pediatric/)**: Updated guidelines emphasizing health and quality of life

### Continuing Education
Self-study modules, online learning portals ([Pedagogy](https://cps.ca/en/pedagogy), PREP), national review programs, and podcasts
